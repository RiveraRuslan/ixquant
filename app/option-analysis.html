Option Analysis

Our Option Simulator is a powerful and intuitive set of tools to support option traders. It has been designed based on trader feedback and provides critical insights into your option trades analysis. It will certainly become your main analytical instrument offering a range of valuable features that will give you an advantage in your option trading.

When using Option Simulator you will be able to:

Make quick and unlimited simulations of your option trading strategies

Create personalized portfolios

Measure position risk

Create multi-leg and multi-expiration option trading setups

Build combined option strategies

Visualise results with the Payoff chart

Save the setup (strategy) for future use

Manage the database of the underlying instruments

Replicate the real portfolio positions.

Questions to be answered:

Trading options is often a challenging task. A number of parameters are involved, such as implied volatility, option Greeks, the price of the underlying instrument that an option trader will have constantly to monitor, and which will directly impact the option prices and therefore your overall open positions.

To keep risks under control and take well-timed trading decisions, you must be able to attain answers to the following questions:

Our Option Analysis tools will quickly provide you with these answers and support you in your option trading 24/7.

What is the current directional bias of my option positions? In other words, what is your overall delta?

How would a change in implied volatility or price fluctuations of the underlying instrument impact my Greeks and PNL?

Is time decay favourable for my overall positions, and to what extent?

What will my profitability range be at the expiration or at a specific date?
